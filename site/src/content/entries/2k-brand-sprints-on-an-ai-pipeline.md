## The how

1. Day 1: intake call becomes a structured creative brief via a Claude-assisted questionnaire.
2. Overnight: ~300 directional explorations across logo territory, type pairing, and palette — batch-generated, then auto-contact-sheeted.
3. Day 2: the designers kill 290 of them. The surviving 10 get human refinement in Figma.
4. Day 3–4: three directions presented; client picks; refinement.
5. Day 5: guidelines PDF generated from a master template, copy drafted by Claude against the brief, edited by the founders.
6. Numbers shared: $2K price, ~14 hours of human time per sprint, ~60% margin, 11 sold in Q1.

## Run it back

The pipeline is replicable in a week. The defensible part is the kill rate — clients pay for the 290 noes.
