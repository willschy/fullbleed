**What it is.** Hera Launch is a web tool that generates product launch videos from a text prompt. You describe what you are launching, and it produces a finished video with motion design, typography, and timing already baked in. The model is opinionated, meaning it makes those production decisions for you rather than handing you a blank timeline to figure out.

**Why it matters.** A polished launch video normally means a motion designer, a brief, rounds of feedback, and a week or two of calendar. Hera compresses that into something closer to ten minutes. For a product team that ships often, or a solo creative who needs something credible without the overhead, that gap is real.

**How to use it.** Write a prompt that describes your product and the tone you want. Hera returns a video with its own choices about pacing and type treatment. Watch it through and decide if those choices serve your launch. If the motion feels too fast or the hierarchy reads wrong, you can adjust from there, though the tool is steering you toward accepting its defaults rather than rebuilding from scratch. When it lands where you need it, export and drop it into your Product Hunt post, your landing page, or wherever the launch is living.

**Limits.** Because Hera makes the design calls for you, you are working within its aesthetic range. If your brand has specific motion guidelines or a very particular typographic voice, you may find yourself fighting the tool rather than finishing faster. The output quality is also worth testing on your actual product before you commit to a subscription, since AI-generated motion design can read as generic when the subject matter is complex or visually specific. There is not much public detail yet about export options, resolution, or how much you can override once the first version is generated.
